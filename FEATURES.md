# KrishiSaarthi Platform Features

A comprehensive list of agricultural intelligence and management features available in KrishiSaarthi.

## 🛰 Satellite Analysis & Field Monitoring
- **Dual Vegetation Indexing**: Real-time EVI (Enhanced Vegetation Index) and NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) monitoring.
- **Smart Crop Health Scoring**: Automated health scoring based on multi-spectral satellite imagery and manual field logs.
- **Interactive Multi-Field Mapper**: Draw and manage multiple field polygons with precise geographic coordinates.
- **AWD (Alternate Wetting and Drying) Reports**: Specialized assessment of irrigation practices to optimize water usage and carbon sequestration.

## 🤖 AI-Powered Planning
- **Crop Rotation Planner**: AI-suggested rotation cycles tailored to specific seasons (Kharif, Rabi, Zaid) and field conditions.
- **Yield Prediction**: Machine learning models that estimate harvest production based on local weather results and historical performance.
- **Automated Alerts**: Real-time notifications for critical agricultural events (e.g., predicted pest outbreaks or scheduled irrigation).

## 💰 Financial & Resource Management
- **Integrated P&L Tracker**: Visual profit and loss dashboards that break down expenses vs. income for each field.
- **Carbon Credit Sequestration**: Monitor and estimate carbon credits earned through sustainable farming practices like AWD.
- **Budgeting & Accounting**: Comprehensive tools for managing farm budgets and tracking real-time profitability.
- **Logistics Tracking**: Managed inventory, labor, and equipment booking systems.

## 🌤 Weather & Environmental Insights
- **7-Day Forecast Integration**: Localized weather data connected directly to field locations.
- **Soil Health Guidance**: Context-aware advice for fertilizing and maintaining soil structure based on the current crop cycle.

## 💬 Smart AI Assistant
- **KrishiAssistant**: An integrated chat interface for real-time agricultural troubleshooting and platform navigation.
- **Context-Aware Support**: The AI understands your currently selected field and provides relevant advice.
